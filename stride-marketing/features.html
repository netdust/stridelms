Features

Everything you need to manage in-person training

Editions & Sessions

One course, many editions. Schedule the same training multiple times with different dates, venues, and capacities.

  • Multiple sessions per edition
  • Venue & capacity management
  • Status: open, full, cancelled, postponed
  • Speaker assignments

Smart Enrollment

Three ways to enroll:

Individual — Self-service signup
Colleague — Manager enrolls team members
Trajectory — Multi-course program enrollment

  • FluentForms integration
  • Automatic capacity updates
  • Waitlist support
  • Cancellation rules (14-day policy)

Quotes & Vouchers

Auto-generate quotes on enrollment. Apply vouchers. Export to accounting.

  • PDF quotes with your branding
  • Member, action, speaker vouchers
  • Exact Online CSV export
  • VAT validation

Attendance & Completion

Track who showed up. Trigger certificates automatically.

  • Per-session attendance
  • Hours calculation
  • Completion rules (%, count, all)
  • LearnDash certificate integration

Two Dashboards

User Dashboard

  • My courses
  • My quotes
  • Trajectory progress
  • Calendar view
  • Profile management

Admin Dashboard

  • Edition overview
  • Student list
  • Attendance panel
  • Bulk actions
  • Exports (CSV, PDF)
  • Email panel

Trajectories

Multi-year learning paths. Required + elective courses. Visual progress tracking.

  • Group courses into programs
  • Set completion requirements
  • Track progress over years
  • Auto-graduate on completion
