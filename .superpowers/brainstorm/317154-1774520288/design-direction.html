What Changes Where

tokens.css
All 40+ custom properties remapped to editorial palette. Colors, typography, shadows, border-radius, spacing. This is the swappable layer.
components.css
Buttons, cards, badges, forms, navigation — all reference tokens. Mostly untouched structurally. Add editorial tweaks: rounded-full buttons, no-border cards, glass nav.
front-page.php
New structure. Editorial hero, course cards, mission section, testimonial, CTA. Content pages like this are client-swappable via mu-plugin.

Healthcare Demo Flavor

The editorial warmth works perfectly for healthcare: it says "we care about your people's growth" rather than "here's another enterprise tool." The teal primary has strong healthcare associations. The warm paper surfaces reduce the clinical feel that plagues most LMS platforms.

  • Dutch (nl_BE) labels stay — "Opleidingen", "Inschrijven", "Mijn account"
  • Demo content: healthcare-appropriate course titles (e.g., "Palliatieve Zorg", "Eerste Hulp bij Psychische Problemen")
  • Serif headlines feel authoritative for medical/professional context
  • Warm surfaces signal approachability for care workers
