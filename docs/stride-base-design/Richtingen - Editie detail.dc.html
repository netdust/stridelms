Stride · visuele reskin · stap 1 van 3
Drie richtingen, één proefscherm

What's fixed in every direction: the warm-paper surfaces, the light overall key, Newsreader as the editorial serif accent, badge semantics, and the token-driven setup. The IA, page anatomy and flows are untouched — this is purely the visual layer.

What varies per direction: the primary hue, the interface sans (replacing Plus Jakarta Sans), and the surface language — how cards, rows and panels separate themselves (soft shadow vs. flat surface-shifts vs. hairline + crisp).

Proof screen: the in-person edition detail (/edities/…) at desktop width — it combines badges, tabs, session rows and the enrollment panel in one view.

How to judge: react to color feel, type voice and surface language. States, mobile and motion get full treatment once a direction is chosen — mixing is allowed (e.g. "palette of 2, surface language of 3").
